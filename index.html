Welcome to Earth Plus

Our mission is to protect endangered species and their environments across the world.

Due to various reasons such as habitat loss, pollution, invasive species, human interference, and more, several species have been brought near the brink of extinction, with their populations falling over the years.

At Earth Plus, we aim to resolve this issue as soon as possible, for the sake of the endangered species.
